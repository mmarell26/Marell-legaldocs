Privacy Policy

This page explains how Marell LegalDocs collects, uses, and protects information submitted through the website and intake form.

1) Information We Collect
We may collect information you submit through the intake form or communications, including names, contact information, case-related details you provide, and files you upload.

2) How We Use Information
Information is used solely to prepare documents requested by the client, communicate about the intake, and deliver the completed documents.

3) Sharing of Information
We do not sell your information. We may share information only as necessary to operate the service (for example, using third-party tools for form intake, email delivery, or file storage), or if required by law.

4) Data Storage and Security
We take reasonable steps to protect information. However, no method of internet transmission or storage is completely secure. You are responsible for submitting accurate and appropriate materials.

5) Client Responsibility
Do not submit information you are not comfortable sharing through online systems. If your matter is highly sensitive, consider consulting a licensed attorney.

6) Updates
This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of any changes.
