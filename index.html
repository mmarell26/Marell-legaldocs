Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers, strict boundaries, and no confusion.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. Marell LegalDocs is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice, legal strategy, or legal opinions. We prepare documents based on client-provided information and instructions.

Can you tell me what I should file?

No. We do not advise clients on what to file. If you need guidance on legal strategy or what filing is appropriate, consult a licensed attorney.

Will you file the documents for me?

No. Clients are responsible for filing, service, deadlines, and compliance with court rules. We deliver documents electronically for you to file.

What do you need from me to start?

Complete the secure intake and upload any relevant paperwork (court notices, prior filings, drafts, and supporting materials). The accuracy of your document depends on the completeness of your information.

Do you guarantee the outcome of my case?

No. We do not guarantee outcomes or court decisions. Court acceptance and results depend on many factors outside document preparation.

What if I need a correction after delivery?

If the information you provided was incomplete or changes are needed, you can request an update through intake. Turnaround and any fees depend on the scope of changes.

What courts do you serve?

Services are intended for matters filed in Pennsylvania courts, including Philadelphia County and surrounding Pennsylvania jurisdictions.
