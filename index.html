Practice Overview

A court-adjacent document preparation service designed to help pro se litigants submit paperwork that is clear, organized, and filing-ready—without crossing into legal advice.

Client Eligibility

Clear boundaries protect you and keep the service professional and predictable.

Appropriate if you:

  • Are representing yourself (pro se)
  • Know the document you need to file (or have court instructions)
  • Want professional formatting and clear structure
  • Can provide accurate facts, dates, names, and supporting materials

Not appropriate if you need:

  • Advice on what to file or what arguments to make
  • Legal strategy or legal analysis
  • Representation or court appearances
  • Someone to communicate with the court on your behalf
