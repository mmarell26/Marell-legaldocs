FAQ

Clear answers before you start.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. Marell LegalDocs provides non-attorney document preparation only. We do not provide legal strategy, advice, or representation.

Do you file or serve documents for me?

No. Filing and service remain the client’s responsibility unless you hire a licensed attorney or authorized process server.

What affects turnaround time?

Document type, length, and intake completeness. Missing facts/exhibits pause the turnaround clock until received.

What counts as a revision?

Formatting/structure corrections within the included revision tier. New facts/arguments/exhibits require new scope and a new invoice.

What if I have an urgent hearing?

If you are facing an urgent hearing or complex legal issues, consult a licensed attorney as soon as possible.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Professional terms that match how document prep work is actually performed (time-based review + drafting).

1) Intake Review (Before Drafting)

If you cancel after intake review but before drafting, any refund (if approved) will be reduced by the value of intake review time already completed.

2) Drafting Started

Once drafting begins, the service becomes time-based and non-refundable in most situations because work product is being created.

3) Revisions

Revisions are limited to formatting and structure within the applicable revision tier. New facts, new arguments, or new exhibits are treated as new scope and require a new invoice.

4) Court Outcomes

Marell LegalDocs does not guarantee court acceptance, filing approval, or legal outcomes. Final determinations are made solely by the court.

5) Disputes

By submitting payment, the client acknowledges and agrees to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.
