FAQ

Clear answers before you start.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. We provide non-attorney document preparation only. We do not provide legal strategy, advice, or representation.

Do you file or serve documents for me?

No. Filing and service remain the client’s responsibility unless you hire a licensed attorney or authorized process server.

What affects turnaround time?

Document type, length, and intake completeness. Missing facts or exhibits pauses the turnaround clock until received.

What counts as a revision?

Formatting/structure corrections within the selected revision tier. New facts/arguments/exhibits require new scope and a new invoice.
