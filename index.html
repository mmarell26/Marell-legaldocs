FAQ

Are you a law firm?

No. Marell LegalDocs is a non-attorney document preparation service. We do not provide legal advice or representation.

Do you file documents with the court for me?

No. You are responsible for filing, paying court fees, and serving all parties in accordance with court rules and deadlines.

How do I get started?

Click “Start Intake,” complete the questions, and provide your details. If exhibits are needed, you can upload them through intake (or we’ll request them by email).

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround depends on the document type, length, and how complete your intake is. You can request rush service through intake when available.

If you are facing an urgent hearing or complex legal issues, consider consulting a licensed attorney.
