Refund & Cancellation Policy

Marell LegalDocs provides non-attorney document preparation services. Because our services involve time-based review and custom document preparation, the following refund and cancellation terms apply.

Intake Review. Submission of an intake form does not guarantee acceptance of service. If a request is declined prior to payment, no charges apply.

Payments. Payment is required before drafting begins. Once payment is received and work has started, fees are non-refundable.

No Refunds After Drafting Begins. Due to the nature of document preparation services, no refunds are issued once drafting has commenced, regardless of filing outcome, court acceptance, or changes in client circumstances.

Client Cancellations. If a client cancels after payment but before drafting has begun, a partial refund may be issued at Marell LegalDocs’ discretion, less any administrative or review time already incurred.

Revisions. Revisions are limited to formatting and structural corrections within the applicable revision tier. Requests that exceed revision scope, introduce new facts, or require additional documents are treated as new services and require a new invoice.

Court Outcomes. Marell LegalDocs does not guarantee court acceptance, filing approval, or legal outcomes. All filings are submitted pro se, and final determinations are made solely by the court.

Disputes. By submitting payment, the client acknowledges and agrees to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.
