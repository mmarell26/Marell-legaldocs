Services & Pricing

We prepare court-ready documents based on the information you provide. Pricing is based on complexity, length, exhibits, and turnaround. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

How pricing works: Choose the tier that matches your document. Final price is confirmed after intake, especially if there are multiple parties, multiple issues, heavy exhibits, or same-day deadlines.

$950+ (multi-document packets + indexing + exhibit management)

Turnaround

Rush: Same-day (if accepted) (+$300)
After-hours: After 6 PM (+$150 additional)

Rush is based on availability and intake completeness. If your intake is missing key facts, exhibits, or captions, turnaround pauses until we have what we need.

Revisions & Scope Rules

Included revisions:
Tier 1: 1 revision within 48 hours of delivery
Tier 2: 2 revisions within 72 hours of delivery
Tier 3–4: 2 revisions within 7 days of delivery

What counts as a revision: correcting formatting, fixing typos, updating names/dates, or swapping exhibits.
What does NOT count as a revision: adding new events, new claims, new requests for relief, new parties, or major restructuring after delivery. That is treated as a new document or tier upgrade.
