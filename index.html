Policies & Disclosures

Important Disclosure (Non-Attorney)

Marell LegalDocs is a document preparation service and is not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice, interpret the law, or represent clients in court. All services are limited to document preparation and formatting based on the information you provide.

Delivery Times

  • Standard: 2–3 business days after completed intake (and payment once enabled).
  • Rush Add-On: 24 hours (when available).
  • What you receive: A draft delivered by email (PDF/Word, as applicable) for your review.

Refund & Dispute Policy

  • Work begins after a completed intake (and payment once enabled).
  • Fees are earned upon commencement of drafting/formatting.
  • If a client cancels before work begins, a refund may be issued minus any processing fees.
  • If work has started, refunds are generally not available due to time and labor already performed.

Client Responsibility

  • You are responsible for filing, service, and court deadlines.
  • We rely on the information you provide; incomplete or inaccurate details may affect the output.
  • We can revise formatting and structure based on your feedback, within the scope of the purchased service.
